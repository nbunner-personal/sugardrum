The music blog Music Gleaner recently reviewed my EP 3 Penny Postcard on their website. Here's an extract:

...what this EP does strongly is put forward a very actively interesting singer-songwriter persona; things are never too similar and the sprinklings of alternative genres spices up the music. The incredibly minimal drumming in Choccy Roles and Forgotten are killer examples of using extra instruments to beef out the song, without making it seem too much or too tacky. This sort of multi-instrumentation is very reminiscent of acts like Sufjan Stevens and even to a slight degree, Beck...
